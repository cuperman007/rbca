In a competitive business market we are committed to providing accurate, reliable and timely advice on all matters relating to accountancy and taxation to individuals and businesses. RB Chartered Accountant offers a variety of services invaluable to the growth of your wealth.

 

At RBCA we aim to build strong relationships with our clients. We feel we can achieve this providing high quality services which are focused towards the particular needs of each individual client.

The following working practices are implemented to ensure the best possible service is provided:

  • All work is given a deadline and agreed with the client.
  • All phone messages are returned within 24 hours of them being received.
  • All client correspondence is answered within two days.
  • All client emails are answered within 24 hours.

RB Chartered Accountant is a sole practitioner firm established by Richard Barnett established in 2006.

The registered office is in Penkridge, Staffordshire.

Authorised to practice by the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales
